    Brief introduction to PTC#

    PTC is a discretionary trading system,[1] where you imagine market sentiment as a fluid.

    CVN can be used to describe fixed range volume profiles. This can be imagined similar to thermohaline circulation.

    FTLE can be used to search for LCS.

    You can use the tools(link to tools) category for details on various drawing tools for CVN and FTLE

    This concludes the brief introduction to PTC.

    What is PTC?#

    Price Time Continuum (PTC) is a discretionary trading system.[1]

    How does it work?#

    PTC takes a mereological nihilist approach to trading.[6]

    Where to start?#

    We can start with the weak anthropic principle (WAP)[2](need to add to glossary). Then apply WAP to markets and market-like social structures(add market to glossary). This can be done beyond human societies, including chimps, bonobos, ants, aphids, cleaner fish and others.[3] Even trees trade resources in market-like social structures.[5]

    The field of comparative behavioural economics investigates decisions about the acquisition and exchange of goods and services. It does so in both humans and other species.[4] PTC looks for patterns in the discisions of economic agents(probably should be glossary).
