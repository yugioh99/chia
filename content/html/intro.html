    What is CTS?

    Chia Trading System (CTS) is a discretionary trading system. It's inspired by econophysics to an extent, but it's mostly technical analysis.

    In regards to the name, you could imagine a pile of chia seeds as symbolic of n-dimensional vector space to describe a lattice gauge field of a price time continuum, with hyperbolic geometry of Riemannian manifolds to interpret the spatial relationships of auction mechanics on a cartesian graph. Market sentiment can be thought of as an intersubjective non-newtonian fluid within this price time continuum.

    CTS is focused on using a variety of tools and techniques to modify brain regions responsible for processing visual motion and coordination, to interpret intersubjective phenomenon in markets as fluid dynamics.
