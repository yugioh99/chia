Channel ⓘ
(cha)

TL;DR

A channel is a chart pattern showing price movement between two parallel lines, a "ceiling" (resistance) and a "floor" (support).

Deep Dive

A channel is a chart pattern showing price movement between two parallel lines, a "ceiling" (resistance) and a "floor" (support), indicating a clear trend (up, down, or sideways) where traders buy at support and sell at resistance, or wait for a breakout. Channels help visualize support/resistance, volatility, and predict potential buy/sell points or breakouts, with common types being ascending (uptrend), descending (downtrend), and horizontal (range-bound).

In PTC, channels are analyzed as possible vortices in market sentiment. You can use LGR and FTLE to analyze market sentiment as a fluid.
