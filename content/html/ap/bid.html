bid-ask spread ⓘ
(bid)

TL;DR

The bid-ask spread is the distance between the best bid and best ask.

  • A bid is a buy limit order.
  • An ask/offer is a sell limit order.

Deep Dive

The bid-ask spread is based on limit orders, as it's the difference between the highest buy limit buyers are willing to pay (bids) and the lowest sell limit sellers will accept (asks / offer) for immediate sale, with these prices forming from the limit order book (LOB) where traders place orders at specific prices, influencing liquidity and the spread's width. A high volume of diverse limit orders creates a narrow, liquid spread, while fewer orders lead to a wider spread.
