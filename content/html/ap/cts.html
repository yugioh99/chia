CTS ⓘ
(cts)

TL;DR

Chia Trading System (CTS) is a discretionary trading system. It's inspired by econophysics to an extent, but it's mostly technical analysis.

Deep Dive

CTS has several parts that work together.

Volume Profile to find important price levels in the past. You can use CVN to mark important levels.

VWAP and fixed range VWAP to see where the average transaction volume is.

Channels and line work to find patterns in price action.

DOM and heatmap to see where limit orders are.

Spotgamma to see what the options market is doing.
